Smokey the Cat

A very loving cat who brought happiness to all around her.

Smokey relaxing in bed and looking very wise in her twilight years.

Here are some facts about her

  • She was born sometime around 2005
  • In 2013 Smokey moved in with John-Henry, where she lived with for most of the rest of her life
  • She is one of the few cats who likes water. She would sometimes nap in the bathroom sink or the shower
  • Smokey is a very shy cat by nature, but once she gets to know you she is extremely affectionate
  • Smokey loves the smell of people food
  • Smokey has a beautiful coat of fur, and she sheds a lot
  • She is a very talkative cat. When she wants attention she will tell you so
  • In 2021 she went to live with John-Henry's mother, Paula, where she lived out the rest of her life in comfort
  • In the spring of 2022 Smokey passed away due to old age. She was almost 18 years old
  • If Smokey could talk, she would say that she has had a very happy and full life

"Your cat chooses you forever. Even when they are gone, their memory lives on."
